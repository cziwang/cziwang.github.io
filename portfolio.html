SWEET TWEET

Lonely and socially-deprived in era plagued by COVID-19, we were reminded of what matters most in our life - the friends and family that we hold close to our hearts. Thus, we created SweetTweet, a mobile application that promotes interactions between tight-knit friend groups. We are working to provide a unified platform that users can enjoy generated tweets, text, and organize plans on.

Technical Details: Figma and Adobe Photoshop to design a mobile platform. Utilizes Tweepy's Twitter API and KNN/matrix factorization models to stream personalized tweets using user engagement history.



MOTIVATION
Entertainment and media are always better when consumed with our friends and close circles. SweetTweet is a mobile iOS platform that allows friends to plan events, have organized channels for discussion, share flicks, and recommended media all in one place. We hope to eliminate the constant re-scheduling that goes into hangouts and have everything that a friend group needs in one application.
